The Process

Everything handled,
start to finish.

My process is designed to be completely hands-off for you. I manage every detail — from the first visit to the final payout.

01
Meet & Assess

We meet to determine which items are saleable based on condition and current market demand. I bring my expertise — you bring the coffee.

02
Research & Price

I conduct a thorough retail and resale comp analysis and deliver a detailed pricing spreadsheet. You choose the strategy that feels right for you.

03
Stage & List

I photograph, stage, and list every item across three platforms — handling all buyer inquiries, negotiations, and weeding out scammers, so you never have to.

04
Sell & Pay Out

I coordinate and attend every showing and pick-up. You receive cash payouts on a pre-determined schedule, plus full transparency at every step.
